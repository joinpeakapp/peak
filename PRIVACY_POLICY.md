# Privacy Policy for Peak

**Last Updated: January 30, 2026**

## Introduction

Peak ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Peak mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Peak is designed with privacy in mind. All your workout data is stored **locally on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers. This includes:

- **Workout Templates**: Your custom workout routines, exercises, sets, reps, and rest timers
- **Workout History**: Records of completed workouts, including dates, durations, and performance data
- **Personal Records**: Your best performances for each exercise
- **Streak Data**: Your workout consistency tracking
- **Photos**: Workout photos you take within the app (stored locally)
- **Settings**: Your app preferences, notification settings, and language preferences

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Health data beyond what you manually enter
- Payment information
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Analytics or usage data

## How We Use Your Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- **Workout Tracking**: To display your workout history, progress, and statistics
- **Notifications**: To send you local reminders for scheduled workouts (if enabled)
- **App Functionality**: To provide core features like rest timers, exercise tracking, and personal records

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: All data is stored on your device using secure iOS storage mechanisms
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not sync your data to any cloud servers
- **No Third-Party Access**: Your data is never shared with third parties
- **Device Security**: Your data security depends on your device's security settings (passcode, Face ID, etc.)

## Data Backup and Transfer

- **iCloud Backup**: If you have iCloud backup enabled on your device, your Peak data may be included in your device backups
- **Device Transfer**: When you transfer to a new device, your data will transfer if you restore from a backup
- **Manual Export**: You are responsible for backing up your data through device backups

## Permissions We Request

Peak may request the following permissions:

- **Camera**: To take workout photos (optional, only when you choose to take a photo)
- **Photo Library**: To save or select workout photos (optional)
- **Notifications**: To send workout reminders (optional, can be disabled in settings)

All permissions are optional and only requested when needed for specific features.

## Children's Privacy

Peak is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

## Changes to Workout Data

- **User Control**: You have full control over your data and can delete workouts, exercises, or clear all data at any time
- **Data Deletion**: Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data from your device
- **No Recovery**: Since we don't store data on servers, deleted data cannot be recovered

## Third-Party Services

Peak does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline except for:

- **App Store**: For app downloads and updates (managed by Apple)
- **System Services**: Standard iOS system services for app functionality

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Modify**: Edit or update your workout data at any time
- **Delete**: Remove individual workouts or clear all data
- **Export**: Backup your data through device backups
- **Control**: Manage app permissions in your device settings

## Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

- Apple App Store Guidelines
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

## Data Retention

- **Active Use**: Data is retained on your device as long as you use the app
- **App Deletion**: All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app
- **No Server Storage**: We do not retain any data on our servers

## International Users

Peak can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data remains in your country and is subject to your local data protection laws.
